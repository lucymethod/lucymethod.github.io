30/30

People who want to learn more about Lucy Method can contact us to schedule a 30/30 Consultation.
The practitioner will get in touch with you, and provide further information regarding our free 30/30 Consultation and pricing.
In twice 30 minutes, we perform a complete movement analysis, at a time and place convenient for you.
However, make sure that you have plenty of space to move. You’ll need it.
Public parks in Amsterdam seem to work very well.

This thorough analysis measures several different individual aspects will be analyzed, like:

- Anatomy
- Movement
- Mobility
- Endurance
- Performance
- Daily life
- Social behavior & habits
- Nutrition
- Cessation
- Goals
- Results

In the following days after the 30/30 Analysis has taken place, the practitioner will develop a custom 12 week method program, no this isn’t just another exercise routine.
Lucy Method will be customized accurately to your specific needs found after the 30/30 Analysis.
From here on you will work on an estimated 12 week based program together with the practitioner.
The optimal number of sessions per week will be determined during the 30/30.
