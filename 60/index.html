60/60

Humans finding Lucy Method appealing can contact our platfrom.
The practitioner will arrange a consulatation-meeting which is free of charge.

Consultation is named 60/60 which stands for:
 60 minutes intake.
60 minutes movement analysis.

During the intake, the coach will go through several personalized aspects, like:
 - Anatomy
- Movement
- Endurance
- Performance
- Daily life
- Social behavior & habits
- Nutrition
- Cessation
- Goals & results

Followed with 60 minutes of movement analsysis which will be held in an open-air enviorment or adapted to certain environmental demands of the client.
In the following days after the 60/60 has taken place, the method will be customized to a program according to the specific needs of the human.
Due to the ability of measurment and achievment, the Lucy Method program is being applied in an essential estimated timeframe of 12 weeks.
The amount of sessions per week will be discussed during the 60/60.

Circumstances in a human's life may disable the human to follow a 12 week program in Lucy Method.
Therefore possibilities of having single sessions in Lucy Method are present.
