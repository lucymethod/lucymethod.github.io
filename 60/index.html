60/60

Humans finding Lucy Method appealing can contact the platform.
The platform will get in touch with you by email providing further information regarding consultation and financial charge.
After approval the practitioner applying the method will arrange a consultation-meeting which is free of charge.
The meeting will be held at an environment of choice.

60 minutes intake.
 60 minutes movement analysis.
 During the 60 minutes intake, the practitioner will go through several personalized aspects, like:

- Anatomy
- Movement
- Mobility
- Endurance
- Performance
- Daily life
- Social behavior & habits
- Nutrition
- Cessation
- Goals
- Results

Followed with 60 minutes of movement analysis which will be held at an open-air environment or adapted to certain environmental demands of the certain human.
In the following days after the 60/60 has taken place, the practitioner will customize the method accurately to the specific needs of the certain human.
Which will result in a 12 week programme based on Lucy Method.
