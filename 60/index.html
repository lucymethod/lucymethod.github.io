60/60

Humans finding Lucy Method appealing can contact our platform.
The practitioner applying the method will arrange a consultation-meeting which is free of charge.

60 minutes intake.
60 minutes movement analysis.

During the 60 minutes intake, the practitioner will go through several personalized aspects, like:
 - Anatomy
- Movement
- Mobility
- Endurance
- Performance
- Daily life
- Social behavior & habits
- Nutrition
- Cessation
- Goals
- Results

Followed with 60 minutes of movement analysis which will be held in an open-air environment or adapted to certain environmental demands of the human.
In the following days after the 60/60 has taken place, the method will be customized to the specific needs of the human.
Which will result in an 12 week program based on Lucy Method.

Circumstances in a human's life may disable the human to follow a 12 week program in Lucy Method.
Therefore possibilities of having single sessions in Lucy Method are present.
